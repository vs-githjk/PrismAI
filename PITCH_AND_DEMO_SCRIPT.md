## 1. Core Positioning

### One-line version
PrismAI turns messy meetings into structured intelligence, then keeps the memory alive across follow-ups, decisions, owners, and recurring blockers.

### Short product description
PrismAI is a meeting intelligence system that does more than summarize one conversation. It turns transcripts into action items, decisions, sentiment, follow-up drafts, calendar suggestions, and meeting health, then tracks recurring patterns across meetings so teams do not lose context every week.

### Stronger category framing
PrismAI is not just a meeting summarizer. It is a post-meeting operating system for accountability and memory.

## 2. What Makes PrismAI Different

### Simple differentiation
Most tools stop at transcription and summaries.

PrismAI goes further:
- structured outputs instead of just notes
- live multi-agent analysis
- account-scoped meeting memory
- recurring blocker and decision detection across meetings
- accountability signals like ownership drift and action hygiene

### Versus Otter / Fireflies / generic meeting bots
Otter is strong at capturing meetings.
PrismAI is stronger at turning them into accountable, structured, reusable intelligence.

Otter helps you remember what was said.
PrismAI helps you know:
- what was decided
- who owns what
- what still is not resolved
- what keeps coming back across meetings

### Best short contrast line
Other tools give you meeting records. PrismAI gives you meeting intelligence with memory.

## 3. 30-Second Pitch

PrismAI takes a raw meeting transcript and runs it through seven specialized AI agents in parallel to generate a full operational picture: summary, action items, decisions, sentiment, follow-up email, calendar suggestion, and a meeting health score. But the real difference is that it does not stop at one meeting. PrismAI keeps memory across meetings, so teams can see recurring blockers, resurfacing decisions, and ownership drift instead of rediscovering the same problems every week.

---

## 4. 60-Second Pitch

Every meeting tool claims to save notes. The real problem is that teams still lose context, repeat the same decisions, and forget what actually needs follow-through. PrismAI solves that by turning a transcript into structured intelligence in seconds. It extracts action items, decisions, sentiment, a follow-up draft, a next-meeting suggestion, and a health score. Then, once meetings start accumulating, it detects what keeps resurfacing across them: recurring blockers, unresolved decisions, and overloaded owners. So PrismAI is not just a meeting summarizer. It becomes a memory and accountability layer for the team.

## 7. Best Demo Transcript Characteristics

Use transcripts that include:
- multiple named speakers
- at least 2–4 real action items
- at least 1–2 decisions
- some mild tension or disagreement
- a clear follow-up or next meeting mention

Best themes:
- product launch
- user research review
- roadmap prioritization
- client/project planning

Avoid:
- overly clean or robotic transcripts
- too-short transcripts
- conversations with no decisions or owners

## 9. Objection Handling

### “Isn’t this just another meeting summarizer?”
No. Summarization is only one output. PrismAI structures the meeting into decisions, owners, follow-up artifacts, calendar suggestions, health, and recurring cross-meeting patterns.

### “Why not just use Otter?”
Otter is excellent at capture and transcripts. PrismAI is built more around post-meeting intelligence, accountability, and memory across meetings.

### “Why does cross-meeting memory matter?”
Because most teams do not fail from one bad meeting. They fail from repeated unresolved decisions, repeated blockers, and follow-through drift over time.

### “Do I need to sign in?”
You can try PrismAI without signing in, but signing in unlocks persistence, history, cross-meeting memory, and integrations.
